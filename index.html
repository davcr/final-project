ORGANICS

Our line of American handmade soaps are natural soaps made with uncompromising, quality ingredients.

Made with olive and soy base with the finest and purest true essential oils.

Each of these natural soaps is a generous 4 and 7 oz. square; created with only edible ingredients so they can be safely used on your skin.

These products are made without: parabens, alcohols, sodium laurel sulfate, artifical dyes, synthetic chemicals or foaming agents, coconut oil or palm oil, animal by-products and are never tested on animals.
